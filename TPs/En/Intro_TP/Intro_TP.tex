\chapter{Labs recommendations}
The purpose of these practical sessions is to illustrate the second-year energy conversion course through the study of four experiments:

\begin{itemize}
\item Buck Converter (simulations with LTSpice)
\item Four-Quadrant Chopper
\item Flyback Switching Power Supply
\item Forward Switching Power Supply
\end{itemize}

To prepare for these sessions, it is necessary to know the corresponding course and to have read the complete text of the practical work in order to plan the different stages of the study. In particular, when a comparison with theoretical curves is requested, it is necessary to bring them to the session and to know how to adapt them to the practical work.

The practical work themes are carried out in rotation: it is therefore imperative to know your pair number before coming to the practical work session.
